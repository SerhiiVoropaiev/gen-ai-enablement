How the GenAI Enablement Education Works

GenAI Enablement is an educational program that lasts from 2 to 4 weeks. It includes theoretical and intensive practical sessions, equipping participants with new skills for success with GenAI.

2 hours

Scoping Session

  • Get a clear understanding of AI with our comprehensive introduction
  • Compare AI performance vs. human performance
  • Discover current and future trends in GenAI
  • Share your needs and requirements for course customization

4 hours

Practical Workshop #1 - Creative Work

  • Receive best practices for AI prompting in content creation
  • Learn how to edit and generate images
  • Explore generating and editing voices and music
  • Master the techniques for creating high-quality texts using popular LLMs
  • Acquire the skills for creating engaging AI-driven videos

4 hours

Practical Workshop #2 - Analytical Work

  • Learn how to use LLMs to process PDFs, Word documents, Excel tables, charts, and diagrams
  • Discover how to create clear, compelling reports and presentations
  • Discuss seamless integration approaches and process management

    • * Choose between splitting the practical workshops into 2 sessions or combining them for a 1-day immersive program (8 hours in total)

    ** For the best learning experience, choose between online workshops or a blended format that combines e-learning with interactive sessions.

2 hours

Retro session

  • Takes place 2-4 weeks after the practical workshops
  • Analyze your results and progress
  • Resolve any final queries in our Q&A session
  • Explore continuous learning opportunities and discuss the future integration of GenAI
